Project Design

The idea is to create a tycoon strategy game which involves buying businesses and property while playing against the ever changing economic game environment.

Game Story

Our story origins begin in late 1989... In a time before the .com boom but after people had begun to become excited at the coming millennium. There is a growing electricity in the air and people are becoming greedy consumers. You, *INSERT NAME*, are a poor young person with a little education, $100 in startup capital and a hunger for success. Will you beat the crazy world of economics and supply and demand to forge an empire? The best time to get started was yesterday, the second best time is now...

Game Goals

  • The player should be able to buy and sell businesses and property.
  • The number of businesses will be 28, 7 times 4 pages.
  • The number of items will be 7. These are used to complete and win the game
  • The game time is set at 1 game day = 4 minutes
  • Each 30 seconds a random economic event may take place resulting in new game dynamics and influencing factors in decisions.
  • There should be at least 10 random events for the purposes of the first version of the game, more could be added at a later date.
  • Graphics should be basic but cartoon like enough to carry the theme of a game as opposed to an app.
  • The players goal is to purchase an island, helicopter, yacht, and rolls Royce and if income still exceeds liabilities the player is able to retire and hand over the reigns of their empire.

Expected Experience

As with most tycoon and strategy games careful balancing of the milestone time/interaction time defines how "good" the game is and random factors result in very satisfying game play as achievement will be earned by the gamer who loves problem solving, numbers and learning from their mistakes. The player will win the game when they have earned enough money and have a high enough income to retire with their luxurys. The game will be designed with mobile players in mind and the game will have 8 "screens", one blank for expansion purposes, these will be navigable in a gameworld world that is 900*1500px and the screen view will be 300*500px.

Intended Game Play

The game will be a simple point and click game available on almost all html5-enabled browsers due to being created in PHASER.IO, which is a Javascript library, and created in a resizeable scale to allow game play on both desktop and mobile devices.

The intended game play is that the player collects as many productive businesses and diversifies if they become unprofitable by selling them or weathering the storm. The economic events will influence income of businesses for a period of time.
